Humans are a bit weird. They always ask questions and think too much. They all try to pretend they know what they're doing when no one really has a clue what's going on. Being a human is easy. You're actually doing it right now. Being a good one takes effort though... ugh.

Here are 10 handy tips from fellow humans to keep you on track:

1. Never assume you are correct, and you will never be entirely wrong.

2. Consider everyone's ideas and actions. Their perspectives may help you.

3. We are all products of our environment. Forgive and understand people.

4. Learn about social issues like discrimination and inequality, and speak out.

5. Continuously question your beliefs. Do they negatively affect others?

6. Don't bitch. Use your energy to understand and help situations instead.

7. The human brain is complex and amazing. Use yours. Think for yourself.

8. Make time for what makes you happy and everything else will follow.

9. Help people in small ways. Pick up that pen he dropped. Collect her mail.

– and big ways: maybe donate your next birthday to help give clean water.

10. Be positive when you can. You will make others happier; they'll do so too.

But, most importantly, whenever you can, when you feel like it...

Smile.
